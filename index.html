🌸 Systems Engineering

Systems engineering is a methodical approach used to design and operate systems where all parts work together.

It includes objectives, constraints, and design goals to ensure systems function properly and meet user needs.

🧸 Information Architecture

Information architecture organizes content so users can easily achieve their goals.

It includes structuring navigation based on audience, actions, and content categories.

🎀 User Experience & Design

UX focuses on making websites easy, smooth, and enjoyable.

It uses testing and feedback to improve usability and design quality.

⚠️ Misinformation & Disinformation

Misinformation is false info shared accidentally, while disinformation is shared intentionally.

Always fact-check sources to avoid being misled.
